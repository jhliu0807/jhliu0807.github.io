About Me

I am now a PhD student of the School of Computer Science, Fudan University (supervised by Prof. Ning Gu, mentored by Prof. Tun Lu and Dr. Dongsheng Li). I obtained my Bachelor of Engineering degree in Computer Science and Technology from School of Computing and Artificial Intelligence, Southwest Jiaotong University (SWJTU) and my Bachelor of Science degree in Statistics from School of Mathematics, SWJTU, in 2021. My research interests include 1) data mining, e.g., recommender systems, causal inference; 2) machine learning, e.g., graph neural network, graph signal processing.
